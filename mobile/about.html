About Us

Jordan and Katie married on September 19, 2015 after dating for almost 6 years. There was a great celebration with friends and family in Cincinnati, Ohio, where Katie was raised. Both are very thankful for all of the friends and family that were able to come to the celebration. After a wonderful honeymoon in Ireland, Jordan and Katie started their new life together. After a few months of living in Toledo, Jordan found a new career at Micro Center Electronics that allowed the couple to move back to Columbus where they met for the first time. They have since bought a house where they have been enjoying their time in Columbus with their dog Haley and cat Pip. Katie and Jordan just recently celebrated their first Anniversary realizing just how fast time seems to go by nowadays. But now they have exciting news to share with everyone!
